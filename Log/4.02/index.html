Who's Fault allow you to solve any conflict.

Have you ever been in a situation when you're walking in the street behind someone and he stops wihout any warning, turns out he was the king of the USA and he starts a nuclear war with France, reveals documents saying that the earth is about to be destroyed by aliens, then revives Hitler to ask him where is his spacial lazer as our only chance to save the planet? Well no longer! Thanks to our free software, you only need security camera footage to know with total certainty who's fault was it, thus saving the whole humankind. (Works also with cars so I guess this has a legit use for insurance companies but shh.)
